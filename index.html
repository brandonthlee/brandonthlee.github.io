About Me

Hi! I am Brandon, and I am currently working at Electronic Arts in Vancouver. Aside from coding, I love building small form factor PCs and mechanical keyboards.

Education

When I first started attending university, I orignally planned on pursuing a joint Major in Mathematics and Economics. But after taking an introductory programming course as a degree requreiment at UBC, I've gained a lot of interest in coding. After consulting with my advisor, I decided to pursue a Mathematics degree that is suitable for my interests in applications of mathematics instead.

While taking Computer Science courses at UBC, I developed a strong understanding of algorithms and data structures, databases, object-oriented programming, and machine learning. I have used these skills to code projects. My Mathematics background has not only given me a deep understanding of mathematical concepts such as coding theory, discrete optimization, and linear algebra but it has helped me to tackle complex programming projects with abstract thinking.
